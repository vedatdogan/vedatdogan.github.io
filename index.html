About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Insight SFI Research Centre for Data Analytics, University College Cork, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Optimization. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from University College Cork (2023) with a thesis on "Bayesian Bilevel Optimization" and a Bachelor of Engineering in Mathematical Engineering from Yildiz Technical University, where I graduated with Honors (Top 10 of class).

My research focuses on developing AI-supported decision-making systems using game-theoretic optimization models. I work on solving hierarchical multi-level optimization problems with advanced machine learning techniques, including Bayesian optimization, bilevel optimization, and multi-objective decision-making under uncertainty. Currently, I am extending my work to network representation data and exploring graph neural network (GNN) algorithms for complex optimization challenges.

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence: Diplomacy, military applications, supply chain management, environmental economics, and game-theoretic decision-making systems
  • Machine Learning: Automated machine learning, multi-task learning, meta-learning, and various supervised/unsupervised learning approaches
  • Optimization: Bi-level, Bayesian, black-box, multi-objective, hyperparameter, and global optimization methods

Research

Bayesian Bilevel Optimization

Developing efficient algorithms for hierarchical optimization problems using Bayesian methods and Gaussian processes. This work addresses the challenge of optimizing systems where upper-level decisions must account for lower-level optimization responses.

Graph Neural Networks for Network Analysis

Applying GNN algorithms to network representation data for optimization and decision-making tasks. Exploring how graph structures can be leveraged for complex optimization challenges in network data.

Game-Theoretic Decision Making

AI-supported decision-making systems for diplomacy, military applications, and strategic optimization. Creating algorithms that solve multi-level optimization problems in strategic contexts.
